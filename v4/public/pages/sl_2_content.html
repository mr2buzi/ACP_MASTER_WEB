Women Well-being Hub - Ashiana Community Project

A longstanding charitable organisation based in Sparkbrook with vast experience of delivering health and well-being focussed projects.
Committed to breaking barriers and changing mind-set especially in relation to well-being of women.
The training is supported by the VCSE Health and Wellbeing Fund, part of a partnership programme between Department of Health & Social Care, NHS England and UK Health Security Agency.
